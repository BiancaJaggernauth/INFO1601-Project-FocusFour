Our Story 📖

We've all been there — exam week rolls around and you suddenly realise you've been spending three hours a night on one subject while completely neglecting another. You meant to study more, you thought you were on track, but somewhere between group chats and late-night snacks, the hours just… slipped away.

FocusFour was born from that exact frustration. We're four university students who wanted a tool that was honest, simple, and actually designed with us in mind — not a generic productivity app repurposed for students, but something built from scratch with our study struggles at the centre.

So we built it. A focused, friendly study tracker that helps you log sessions, hit weekly goals, maintain streaks, and finally see where your time is really going. No fluff, no overwhelm — just clarity, consistency, and a little motivation to keep showing up. 🎯
